JobSite!

Welcome to Thomas Fraley's demo Talent Search site!

Django project running on Heroku

Here's what I made. It'll blow your mind.

JobSite is a fictitious company that needed a new talent acquisition page. To solve their hiring woes, I made an oh-so-slick job site for the company. It features a searchable list of jobs (managed by JobSite personnel) to which anyone can apply. The applicant can upload their resume and cover letter in response to a job posting. JobSite personnel can then interact with the applications through the Django admin site.

It runs on Django, using standard templates. See for yourself.

Feel free to browse my source here, or you can click the link in the section header. Included is a README that details how to get the app running on your own local machine.

I made this in three days. Check it out.

With the exception of the initial spooling out of the app and some bug fixes after the fact, all of the meaningful work on this app happened in just 24 hours spread over three days. Heavy use of bootstrap facilitated this, of course. Styling isn't exactly my forte, but I'm getting better at it. Images came from Pexels, the best free online photo site around.
